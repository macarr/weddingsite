Ceremony

The ceremony will begin at 3pm on the patio, weather permitting. Please arrive early so that everyone is seated before the ceremony begins.

Reception

  • 4pm: Bar opens, Alcoholic punch bowl set up. We have a hybrid "toonie bar" setup, where a selection of domestic and premium beers, house wines and basic liquor will be $2 per drink. Specific brands TBD.
  • 4:30pm: Appetizers served. Assorted finger foods of varying degrees of classiness, from bacon wrapped scallops to mini grilled cheese sandwiches.
  • 6pm: Buffet dinner with vegetarian (vegetable primavera in pesto) and vegan (portobello mushroom stack) options. Table wine provided.
  • LATER: Party. Somewhere in here we'll fit speeches, dessert and dancing. Potentially in that order.
  • 10:30pm: Late night food. Poutine bar, coffee and tea, fresh fruit.
